About

Tena koutou katoa. Nau mai haere mai ki toku wharekai. He wheako motuhake o Aotearoa


Welcome to Haukai Restaurant, located in the stunned Bay of Islands, in Aotearoa New Zealand.
Founded by a couple with the interest of both local customers and the variety of tourist who visit New
Zealand from around the world in mind, Haukai Restaurant focuses on providing excellent
Māori and Pakeha food options for all to taste and enjoy (check out our menu!)

Our restaurant offers an extensive drinks menu, with an incredible selection of red
and white wines from some of New Zealands finest wineries (among others), a fantastic
range of beers and cider, plus non-alcoholic drinks!

For millennia, Māori have been the tangata whenua, the indigenous
people of Aotearoa, and therefore Māori culture is an integral part of
life in Aotearoa New Zealand. Here at Haukai Restaurant we hope to involve
our customers in an authentic Māori experience. To do this, not only do we
offer Māori inspired dishes, but we also use karakia for blessing
food. Karakia are Maori incantations and prayers, used to invoke spiritual guidance
and protection. Learn more about traditional Māori foods and Māori culture.

The Bay of Islands is a fantastic place both to live and visit, with sites
such as sites where the famous 'The Lord of the Rings' movies
were filmed nearby.
