Welcome to Haukai Restaurant

Welcome to Haukai Restaurant. Located in the
beautiful Bay of Islands, Aotearoa New Zealand,
we are dedicated to bringing only the freshest and
finest Māori and Pākehā kai to the table.

Our restaurant offers an extensive menu with
many options to suit all. Whether you are an avid
seafood enjoyer, or you prefer a juicy slow cooked
pork belly, Haukai Restaurant has something for everyone
to enjoy.
